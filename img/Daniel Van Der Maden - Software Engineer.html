About Me

Hi there! I am a senior at the University Of California, Berkeley pursuing a B.A. in Computer Science. I am currently finishing up my degree (2 classes remaining) this fall and I am looking to do an experiance building internship at the same time.

From developing Minecraft servers as a young teen to creating computer graphics for business in high school, I have always enjoyed the process of building and showing my creations. This is why Software Development / Engineering appealed so much to me throughout my time in higher education — and has ultimately pushed me to learn a vast skill set in the world of Computer Science. Furthermore, throughout my time at UC Berkeley, I have gained a particular academic interest in Natural Language Processing, Human-Computer Interaction, and Artificial Intelligence.

I also enjoy helping and teaching other people. I spent a semester at UC Berkeley tutoring C, C++, Java, and Python in the EECS department's self-paced center. I also joined a student organization called "Computer Science Mentors" where I mentored a group of 5 students through their lower division courses. Currently, in my free time, I am working on a chatbot program that allows a student to more easily learn how different parameters of a neural network effects the fidelity of a chatbot.

I have spent the majority of my childhood traveling, so in my free time, I enjoy traveling to different countries and experiencing their culture. Also, enjoy working on my car and taking it out to the track every so often.
