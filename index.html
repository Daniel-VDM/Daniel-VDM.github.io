This was a group project for the Efficient Algorithms and Intractable Problems Course at UC Berkeley.

The difficulty comes from this added condition: Groups of children that form a trouble group and are all assigned to the same bus do not have their friendships counted. The script uses a combination of greedy algorithms and local searches.

My groupâ€™s program generated solutions that were in the top 10% of all approximations in the course.
